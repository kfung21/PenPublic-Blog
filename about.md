# About PenPublic

PenPublic helps Americans find careers in the public sector.

## What we do

Government jobs are scattered across hundreds of agency websites — every city, county, state department, transit authority, and school district runs its own, with no consistent format or location. We centralize postings from hundreds of agencies across California, New York, Texas, Florida, and Washington DC into one place.

Alongside the listings, we provide pension formulas for every state, salary data, career paths, and retirement planning tools.

## Why public sector?

Government work pays better than most people assume once pensions, healthcare, and job security are factored in. But career fairs, schools, and LinkedIn all push private sector roles by default, so most people who would benefit from a public sector career never seriously consider one.

## Who it's for

We believe 99% of working Americans would be better off in a public sector job than where they are now — recent grads, mid-career professionals tired of layoff cycles, and current public employees making sense of their pensions.
