# About PenPublic

We're building the platform for America's 150+ million workers seeking economic security

## **Why PenPublic?**

150+ million workers and students seeking job stability don't realize public sector careers often have better overall compensation and work-life balance. We're building the platform that connects them to America's hidden job market while providing the financial services this massive, underserved demographic lacks. Like Handshake meets NerdWallet, but for the country's most recession-proof workforce with 30-year customer relationships.

## **The Massive Market Opportunity**

### **150+ million addressable market:**

- College students at career fairs choosing their life path
- Mid-career professionals burned out by private sector uncertainty  
- Anyone seeking recession-proof careers with real benefits
- 22M current public sector workers optimizing their advantages

Most don't realize public sector work often pays better when you factor in pensions, job security, and work-life balance. These opportunities are hidden across fragmented websites while this massive demographic lacks specialized financial services.

## **Our Platform: Complete Career & Financial Ecosystem**

We've built the only platform serving the entire journey from career discovery to financial freedom:

### **Career Discovery & Transition:**

- 15,000+ public sector opportunities weekly from 200+ public agencies
- Career transition tools for private-to-public sector moves
- Early career engagement capturing decades of service time

### **Financial Services & Benefits:**

- Exclusive partnerships with financial institutions offering public sector worker-focused products
- Pension optimization and security clearance premium planning
- Exclusive user discounts and partner benefits

## **Why We Win:**

- **Lifetime value:** 30+ year relationships vs. competitors' 1-2 year engagement
- **Multiple revenue streams:** Job platform + financial services + B2B data
- **Network effects:** More users = better deals for everyone = more users
- **Defensive moat:** Serving America's most recession-resistant demographic

## **Revenue Streams Built for Scale:**

- **B2B Data & Analytics:** public sector workforce intelligence for contractors, recruiters, HR platforms
- **Financial Services:** Exclusive partnerships with major financial institutions offering public sector-specific products, generating commission-based revenue from our engaged user base
- **Career Services:** Premium tools for career transitions and optimization

## **Our Competitive Advantage:**

- **Handshake:** Serves temporary college students (1-2 years each)
- **NerdWallet:** Generic financial advice for general population
- **PenPublic:** Lifetime relationships with America's most stable, underserved workforce

## **Technical Innovation:**

Our proprietary systems solve the public sector data aggregation problem at scale, making us the #1 public sector job aggregator in California, New York, Texas, Florida, and Washington DC. Our offline-first architecture ensures reliability while we're building financial products around public sector-specific advantages that traditional banks can't or won't serve.

## **Market Timing:**

Economic uncertainty drives demand for stability. public sector hiring continues while private sector cuts back. We're positioned to capture Americans' flight to economic security.

## **The Vision:**

We're becoming the primary platform where millions of Americans discover that stability isn't settling—it's smart. As economic volatility increases, we're the bridge to America's hidden job market with the financial services to maximize it.

From college career fairs to retirement planning, we're building the 30-year relationship that transforms how Americans think about work and financial security.
