# Public Pensions

Understanding public pensions is one of the most compelling reasons to consider a government career. This section covers pension systems state by state — how benefits are calculated, what formulas exist, and the history and labor agreements that shape your retirement.

## California

California has over 80 public pension systems covering 4.6 million members.

- [California Pension Systems](./california) — All systems, 1937 Act counties, independent city pensions, reciprocity, bargaining units, MOUs, and the full SB 400 → PEPRA history
- [California Pension Formulas](./california-formulas) — Every CalPERS and CalSTRS formula, Classic vs PEPRA, and how local agencies choose their formula
- [Retirement Benefits at CSU and UC](./Retirement-Benefits-at-CSU-and-UC-Systems) — CalPERS eligibility at CSU and UCRP at UC
